Home Renovation Report

The Renovation Estimate Report will provide an estimate for the full renovation of your home. We will make a recommendation on which items would increase the value of your home the most. From there, you can choose the items that are important to you.

Benefits from ordering this report:

  • Know what items in your home increase value the most.
  • Know renovation cost for financial planning purposes.
  • Use our estimate in negotiations with other contractors. (if you decide not to go with one of our contractors).
  • Utilize our experience for better work planning of your renovation.
